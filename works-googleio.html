Google I/O 2021

With WFH still in full swing in 2021, we teamed up with Google to create a completely digital, live-streamed I/O event. I worked with Modicum to design and animate content primarily for Alphabet CEO Sundar Pichai's opening Keynote. In between I dabbled in supporting other talk tracks with animation.
