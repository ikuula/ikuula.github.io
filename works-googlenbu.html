Google NBU

Google’s Next Billion Users initiative executes events around the globe, introducing new products and programs in an effort to reach “the next billion” internet users in emerging markets. In 2020, I worked with Modicum to smoothly transition to an all-virtual version of the event. This comprised of a new framework and visual system for live-streamed speakers, a virtual waiting room with branded graphics, blog posts, and social media graphics.

Presentation design, Event branding, Motion framework and toolkit
