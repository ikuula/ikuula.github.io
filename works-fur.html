Fur Typography

Typography created from cut paper, fur, a camera, and a lot of patience. Letterforms based off of Brandon Grotesque, cut from card stock with a fur blanket pulled through to create a wild, messy, animal of a typographic display.

Photography, Typography, Exploration

Photographed in a dark hallway with a few can lights clamped to chairs and a DSLR.
