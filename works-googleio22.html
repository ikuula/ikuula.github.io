Google I/O 2022

In 2022 I worked with Modicum to put on Google I/O, their largest in-person and live-streamed event. My main contribution was designing and animating content for Alphabet CEO Sundar Pichai's opening Keynote. In between I dabbled in supporting other speakers with content animations.
